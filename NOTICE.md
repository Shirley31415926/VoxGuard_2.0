# Third-party components

This demo loads `@mediapipe/tasks-vision` version 0.10.35 from jsDelivr and the official Google MediaPipe Face Landmarker and Pose Landmarker Lite model assets.

MediaPipe Tasks Vision is distributed under the Apache License 2.0. Review the upstream package and model documentation before redistribution or commercial use.

The implementation in this folder is an independent prototype and does not include or claim the trained Digital FAST research model.
