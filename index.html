What this demo measures
  • Face: tilt-normalised vertical difference between the two mouth corners.
  • Arms: wrist level difference and downward movement relative to shoulder width.
  • Speech: microphone energy, voiced duration and pauses. It does not yet detect slurring or aphasia.
Privacy and limitations
  • Inference runs in the browser. This demo contains no upload endpoint or analytics.
  • The browser still captures raw frames and audio in device memory before extracting landmarks and audio energy.
  • Thresholds are heuristic and have not been clinically validated.
  • A real medical model requires consented, clinically labelled acute-stroke and stroke-mimic data.
